Safari tour holidays

If a safari holiday is on your travel bucket list, check out our list of top safari destinations.

An unforgettable experience waits when you fly to one of our inspiring safari destinations. Take a safari tour holiday to experience the wild up close and personal. Safari travel will thrill and delight the whole family.

Experience guided safari tours to see the big five with local experts. Take an African safari to explore the plains of Northern Kenya and see wild cats up close. And trek deep into the jungles of Uganda to witness gorillas in their natural habitat. Take a desert adventure over endless sand dunes to witness the beauty of the Middle East. We recommend taking a photo safari for those once-in-a-lifetime shots.

Get close to wildlife and nature by visiting one of the many national parks and game reserves in our destination network, and watch the wild world go by on your doorstep.
